Welcome to Green Power Company

Building a Greener Tomorrow, Today

Since our creation in 2013 by two young friends passionate about the future of solar energy, we have experienced a remarkable evolution and achieved many successes.


Over the years, we have established successful collaborations with renowned international organizations such as: "GIZ", "US-AID", "Direct-AID" and "UN-ESCWA".

  • WWe are proud to be the first photovoltaic installation company in Tunisia to obtain ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015 and ISO 45001:2018 certifications, demonstrating our commitment to quality, the environment and occupational safety.
  • We are also approved by the National Agency for Energy Management (ANME) and eligible by the Tunisian Electricity and Gas Company (STEG), thus demonstrating our commitment to the highest quality and compliance standards higher.
  • Our team currently has 70 highly qualified employees, including PhD engineers, who put their expertise and passion at the service of each project. In 2017, we created a Research and Development unit to stay at the forefront of technological advances and offer innovative solutions to our customers.
  • Internationally, we provide technical assistance and training on behalf of our partners, contributing thus to the development of skills and the advancement of the solar industry.
  • Do not hesitate to contact us for any request for information or quote. We look forward to working with you to realize your solar projects.
