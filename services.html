process and the services we can serve

Smart & sustainable energy consumption

Study And Advice

GPC conducts a feasibility study to understand the owner's electrical needs, including consumption, equipment, energy goals, and potential savings.

Technicians would provide you with an estimate proposal, project cost, energy savings, and deadlines.

They would offer ,also, opinions on project realization, sizing, and installation constraints.

Design & Follow Up

GPC has invested in its staff by recruiting PhD engineers, engineers and technicians.

This enables it to have a crew that is qualified and trained to development of specifications files, field research, development of preliminary and detailed study of STEG connection.

We serve our clients with respect, integrity, professionalism and rigor.

Installation

We take care of the installation of the appropriate supports on the roof or on the ground to guarantee the stability and the durability of the photovoltaic panels, electrical connection; we make the electrical connections between the panels, the inverters and the electrical system, the panels fixing; the photovoltaic panels will be carefully fixed on the supporting structures, optimizing their orientation and inclination to maximize their exposure to the sun.

Quality

Our quality control team carries out regular inspections to ensure that all our photovoltaic installations meet the strictest quality standards.

From planning to commissioning, each step is thoroughly checked to ensure compliance with internal and external quality standards.





Verification & Customer Training

Our installation team carry out a series of rigorous tests to ensure that each component of the system is correctly installed and operates according to the highest safety and performance standards.

This crucial step ensures that your photovoltaic system will operate optimally, maximizing its solar energy production capacity.

Service After Sale

Our commitment to our customers goes well beyond the initial installation of their photovoltaic panels.

We consider our relationship with you as a long-term partnership, and that is why we have implemented a dedicated phase of After-Sales Service, aimed at ensuring the continued good functioning of your system and meeting your post-sale needs, facility.
